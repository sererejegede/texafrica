Why the Event Xperience Africa?

THE EVENT XPERIENCE AFRICA (TEXA) is an annual conference geared towards equipping an ever growing audience of event entrepreneurs with the right tools needed to thrive in the industry. The conference is targeted at seasoned event professionals and beginners alike empowering them to be more and do more at the same time.

The Event Xperience Africa (TEXA) is all about learning, unlearning and relearning.

The conference brings together event professionals from various parts of Africa for three days of learning, networking, sharing and getting in touch with the latest innovations and trends in the event industry.

The conference provides a platform for attendees to learn from industry experts from around the globe and also form new and lasting relationships with other event professionals.
